Recipe 1: Spaghetti with Tomato Sauce

Ingredients

  • Tomato sauce

  • Mince meat

Instructions

  1. Cook the sauce with mince meat and tomato sauce
  2. Mix the spaghetti and the cooked spaghetti with the mince meat sauce together
  3. Garnish
